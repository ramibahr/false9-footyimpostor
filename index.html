How to play

Find the Impostor

  1. 1Everyone gets the same real footballer, except one impostor (or two, in a bigger group).
  2. 2The impostor secretly gets a similar decoy player instead, enough to bluff with.
  3. 3Pass the phone round. Each player privately taps their own card to see their role.
  4. 4Take turns describing the player without saying the name, then vote out whoever you think is faking it.
